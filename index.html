HOW TO MAKE DARMIE
Ingridients
Step
A bowl


A plate


Water


A spoon


Oil


An egg


Noodles


A pan


A frying pan


First

Pour the water into the pan and then heat it until it boils


Second

Put the noodles into the pan and wait until it's cooked. While waiting for the noodles to cook, we can break the egg into the bowl


Third

If the noodles are already cooked, we can turn of the stove and throw away the water


Fourth

After that put the noodles into the bowl that already contains egg. Don't forget to put the seasoning


Fifth

After that mix until evenly distributed

Sixth

Heat the frying pan and then if it's already hot you can add the oil

Seventh

Add the noodles that we mixed earlier and cook the darmie until it's cooked

Eighth

Move the darmie into the plate and tadaa your darmie is done

Ninth

You can the darmie right away or eat it with sambal
